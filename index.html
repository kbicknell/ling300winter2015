Ling 300: Bayesian inference

Bayesian inference [Ling 300]

Klinton Bicknell /// Winter 2015

Bayesian techniques provide a rational way of combining probabilistic information, enabling sophisticated analyses of scientific datasets and principled models of cognition and language. This course introduces Bayesian techniques from the ground up.
